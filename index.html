Field Tasking Manager

A simple space to coordinate surveys, local knowledge and field mapping work. Create a field plan for your area or perform work such as splitting an area, preparing data for offline use or comparing your data with existing map data.

Explore workflows

Coordination is the key to what we do.

Workflows

Start with the work that needs to happen in the field.

Set up a field plan

Bring together surveys, community knowledge and field mapping tools. Use ODK for surveys, SketchMap for local knowledge and EveryDoor for places in one flow.

Carry out a field task

Complete a small activity without setting up a full plan. Split an area, prepare data for offline work, compare your data with existing map data or create simple tasks for field teams.

Tools in the ecosystem

Field TM connects existing tools instead of replacing them.

ODK and web forms

Structured surveys for many field contexts.

SketchMap

Participatory mapping that captures local knowledge, boundaries, paths and landmarks drawn by community teams.

EveryDoor

Fast capture of places and services during community visits.

ChatMap

Notes and conversation linked to surveys, areas and field activity.

Offline editing tools

Applications that support field mapping and review without a network connection.

Field plan types

Create a plan that fits the way your team works.

Survey centered plan

Begin with an ODK form, pick an area and coordinate survey teams with clear steps and submission tracking.

Participatory mapping plan

Begin with a SketchMap session to capture community knowledge, then attach surveys or other field work.

Light plan for places

Begin with EveryDoor when the focus is on collecting places and services with minimal setup.
