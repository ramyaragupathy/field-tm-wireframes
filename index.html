Field Tasking Manager

Coordinating field surveys, sketch maps, points of interest and offline maps in a single space. Bringing together ODK, SketchMap, EveryDoor, ChatMap and QField into flexible and modular field workflows.

Explore Workflows

Coordination is the key to what we do.

Workflows

Start from the work that needs to happen in the field and then choose the right tools.

Survey led workflow

Design surveys in ODK, share as web forms or on devices and monitor submissions in a simple coordination view.

Map first workflow

Use SketchMap to plan coverage, split an area of interest into tasks and send teams to the field with clear maps.

Places and services workflow

Capture points of interest with EveryDoor and connect them to surveys or follow up mapping where needed.

Offline map workflow

Prepare QField projects for areas with limited connectivity so that teams can work offline and sync when they return.

Tools in the ecosystem

Field TM connects existing tools instead of replacing them.

ODK and web forms

Primary survey tools for structured questions, repeat groups and detailed follow up.

SketchMap

Sketch based planning for coverage, boundaries and context before or after a survey.

EveryDoor

Fast capture of places and services when teams move through the community.

ChatMap

Conversational notes and feedback that stay linked to projects and areas of interest.

QField

Rich offline map projects with geometry editing and advanced layers.

Project creation options

Create projects that match the way teams work in the field.

Survey centered project

Start from an ODK form, select an area of interest and create tasks for survey teams.

Planning centered project

Start from SketchMap, define coverage and then attach surveys or QField projects.

Light project with places

Start from EveryDoor when the focus is on places and services with minimal setup.
